# TripMoja — Original Vision (2023 Bubble.io version)

This is the original product brief from the Bubble.io build. Preserved as reference. The 2026 open source rebuild is guided by `architecture.md` and `open-source-scope.md`, not this document.

Key context: the original platform was launched publicly, featured on LinkedIn (hundreds of likes), appeared on a podcast, and was shut down because solo operation (customer service, dispute resolution, financial risk) was not sustainable for Isaac. The product worked. The business model did not suit a solo founder.

The 2026 rebuild removes the business. It keeps the product.

<!-- Original blurb below -->

TripMoja is a mobile-first long-distance ridesharing platform built specifically for intercity travel in Kenya, starting with the Nairobi-Nanyuki corridor.

The core problem it solves: people are already organizing rides through chaotic WhatsApp groups. Drivers post vague messages like "Leaving for Nairobi at 7am, 2 seats", passengers DM privately, payments happen off-platform, trust is weak, and there is no reputation system, verification, booking flow, or structured discovery.

TripMoja replaces that entire workflow with a structured marketplace.

**Core Product Vision**

- BlaBlaCar adapted for Kenya
- Uber-style UX for scheduled intercity rides
- Community-driven marketplace instead of on-demand taxis
- Optimized for low-trust, WhatsApp-native environments

The product is not ride hailing. It is scheduled rides, shared travel, seat booking, social trust, cost sharing, and intercity mobility.

The emotional pitch: "Your shared journey is one click away."

**Brand Identity**

- Colors: Blue #7697F5, Orange #EF9B5B, Green #9CF1C4
- Visual language: soft rounded UI, clean whitespace, optimistic imagery, human-centered photography, community energy
- Tone: youthful, trustworthy, modern, community-oriented, Kenyan-first
